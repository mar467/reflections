When you're collaborating with other people who need access remotely.

You might want to pull changes manually because otherwise you'll get occasionally automatically updated with changes (from collaborators) you did not want

Branches are to branch off from master branch in case of needing to make structural or whatever changes. Clones are copying onto your local drive. Forks are to copy another's remote onto your GitHub profile.

The benefit of having a copy of the last known state of the remote stored locally (origin/master) is that it enables easier merging
